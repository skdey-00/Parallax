PARALLAX
The Veil cannot be detected directly. Only by aligning your perspective can you reveal the truth. Movement is your weapon. Convergence is your ammunition.
WASD / ARROWS - Move | SPACE / CLICK - Fire | Press H for help
HOW TO PLAY
THE CONCEPT
In Parallax, enemies exist at different depths (distances) in 3D space. Traditional collision doesn't exist. To destroy an enemy, you must align your perspective until the enemy's apparent position matches your targeting reticle.
CONVERGENCE
Move your ship to align with an enemy. Watch the convergence meter at the top of the screen. When it reaches 95%+, a red lock-on ring appears. Fire to destroy.
ENEMY TACTICS
Enemies survive by sliding along the Z-axis — moving closer to or farther from the camera. They don't dodge left or right. You must anticipate their depth changes and re-align.

THE THREE ACTS
ACT I (Waves 1-3): First contact. Basic entities.
ACT II (Waves 4-7): Escalation. Fast Z-dodging, split entities.
ACT III (Wave 8+): Collapse. Sensor interference, maximum threat.
